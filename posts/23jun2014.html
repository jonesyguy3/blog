I currently have them set-up to be viewed on the computer side, until I learn how to make my git a functioning public website.
  • I downloaded Foundation and Bootstrap prebuilt HTML pages to help me create a skeleton for what I need. While I haven't even gotten close to maximizing their use, it did help a ton to copy/paste the HTML skeleton in for my pages. There is a ton more to use and do and hopefully I will get to them in the future
